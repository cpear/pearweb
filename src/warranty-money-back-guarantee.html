Warranty & Money-Back Guarantee

45-Day money-back guarantee

If you decide, for any reason, that you are dissatisfied with the Booster eSaver you may return it for a full refund. Send your request within 45 days of installation to contact-us@boosteresaver.com. An appointment will be made for your vehicle to return to an authorized Integra Performance installer so it may be uninstalled. Failure to arrive at the appointed installation time will result in the guarantee's termination. Integra Performance will work within reason, to accommodate scheduling of Device removal. A $250 fee will be deducted from the amount due to the customer for labor costs and parts replacements.

During this warranty period, if a defect arises in the Device, and you follow the instructions for returning the Device, we will at our option, to the extent permitted by law, either (i) repair the Device using either new or refurbished parts, (ii) replace the Device with a new or refurbished Device that is equivalent to the Device to be replaced, or (iii) refund to you all or part of the purchase price of the Device. This limited warranty applies, to the extent permitted by law, to any repair, replacement part or replacement device for the remainder of the original warranty period or for two years, whichever period is longer. All replaced parts and Devices for which a refund is given shall become our property. This limited warranty applies only to hardware components of the Device that are not subject to fire, alteration, or damage from any third-party repair, third-party parts, commercial use, or other external causes.
